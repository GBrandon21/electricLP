ABOUT US

Our mission is to turn the world over to future generations a cleaner, better and more substantial world. We are doing our part by sharing the benefits and practicallity of electric vehicles. We are covering topics from brands that are already up and running (Tesla) to unkown startups that are contiuning the mission of better cars now.

We reside in colorful Colorado. With the mountains to our west and the plains to our east, we want nothing more that the beauty of this great state to live on for generations and generations for all to enjoy.

Let us be clear, we are not some Grateful Dead listening, tie dye wearing hippies. We are proud Americans who want nothing more than to educate on the bad ass-ness of Electric vehicles.
